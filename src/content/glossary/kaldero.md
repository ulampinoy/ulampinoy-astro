A traditional Filipino cooking pot used for various dishes. It's primarily used for cooking rice but can also be used for boiling.

### Uses in Filipino Cooking

- Cooking rice
- Boiling water
- Making soups
- Cooking stews

Ang sinaing sa apoy ay itinuring na mas masarap.
